PlayStation® 5

(PlayStation 5) The latest Sony PlayStation introduced in November 2020. Powered by an eight-core AMD Zen 2 CPU and custom AMD Radeon GPU, the PS5 is offered in two models: with and without a 4K Blu-ray drive. Supporting a 120Hz video refresh, the PS5 is considerably more powerful than the PS4 and PS4 Pro.

PlayStation® 4

The PlayStation 4 (PS4) is a home video game console developed by Sony Computer Entertainment. Announced as the successor to the PlayStation 3 in February 2013, it was launched on November 15, 2013, in North America, November 29, 2013 in Europe, South America and Australia, and on February 22, 2014 in Japan.
